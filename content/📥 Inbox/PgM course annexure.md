## 1. performance domains

| **Domain**             | **Purpose**                                                                                             | **Key Activities**                                                                                                          | **Key Documents**                                                                                                                                                    | **Key Interactions with Other Domains**                                                                                                                                               |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Strategic alignment    | Ensure the program aligns with the organization's strategic objectives and delivers the expected value. | - Define program vision and objectives.<br>- Align program goals with organizational strategy.<br>- Prioritize initiatives. | - Strategic plan<br>- Business case<br>- Program charter<br>- Program management plan<br>- Program roadmap<br>- Environmental assessments<br>- Program risk strategy | - Interacts with benefits management to ensure the alignment of benefits with strategic objectives.<br>- Engages stakeholder engagement to align stakeholders with strategic goals.   |
| Benefits management    | Maximize the program's benefits and ensure they are realized and sustained.                             | - Define and plan benefits.<br>- Monitor and report on benefits realization.<br>- Optimize benefits delivery.               | - Benefits realization plan<br>- Benefits register<br>- Benefits management plan                                                                                     | - Linked with strategic alignment to ensure benefits support organizational strategy.<br>- Collaboration with lifecycle management to integrate benefits throughout the program.      |
| Stakeholder engagement | Actively manage relationships with stakeholders to ensure their needs and expectations are met.         | - Identify stakeholders.<br>- Analyze stakeholder needs and influence.<br>- Develop and implement engagement strategies.    | - Stakeholder register<br>- Stakeholder engagement plan<br>- Program communications plan                                                                             | - Works with governance framework to *align stakeholder needs with governance policies.*<br>- Supports collaboration to foster a collaborative environment among stakeholders.        |
| Governance framework   | Establish structures and processes to guide program management and decision-making.                     | - Define governance structures.<br>- Establish decision-making processes.<br>- Monitor compliance with policies.            | - Program governance plan<br>- Program management plan<br>- Risk management plan                                                                                     | - Influences stakeholder engagement to ensure governance policies address stakeholder needs.<br>- Provides structure for lifecycle management through defined processes and controls. |
| Collaboration          | Foster a cooperative environment among team members and stakeholders to achieve program objectives.     | - Promote communication and information sharing.<br>- Facilitate team-building activities.<br>- Resolve conflicts.          | - Program level responsibility matrix (RAM)<br>- Team charter<br>- Communication and issue logs                                                                      | - Enhances stakeholder engagement by improving stakeholder relationships.<br>- Supports lifecycle management by promoting teamwork throughout the program phases.                     |
| Lifecycle management   | Manage the program through its phases, from initiation to closure, to achieve program objectives.       | - Program definition<br>- Program delivery<br>- Program closure                                                             | - Program scope<br>- Program schedule<br>- Program budget<br>- Phase gate reviews<br>- Program transition plan<br>- Lessons learned report and register              | - Interacts with benefits management to ensure benefits are delivered at each phase.<br>- Governed by governance framework to ensure compliance with policies and standards.          |

| **Program management plan subsidiary plans**    |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |         |        |
| Benefits Management Plan                        | - Outlines how the program's benefits will be identified, monitored, and realized over time. Includes benefits measurement criteria and tracking mechanisms to ensure the anticipated benefits are achieved and sustained.                                                             |         |        |
| Stakeholder Engagement Plan                     | - Details how stakeholders will be identified, analyzed, and engaged throughout the program. Includes strategies for stakeholder communication, involvement, and managing expectations to ensure support and alignment with program goals.                                             |         |        |
| Governance Plan                                 | - Defines the governance structure for the program, including roles, responsibilities, decision-making authority, and processes for oversight and control. Ensures proper management and accountability throughout the program lifecycle.                                              |         |        |
| Change Management Plan                          | - Describes how changes will be managed within the program. Includes processes for identifying, evaluating, and implementing changes and strategies for communicating and gaining acceptance for changes among stakeholders.                                                           |         |        |
| Communications and Information Management Plan  | - Outlines how information will be collected, managed, and disseminated within the program. Includes communication strategies, channels, and schedules to ensure timely and effective communication among stakeholders.                                                                |         |        |
| Financial Management Plan                       | - Details the program's financial planning and management processes. Includes budgeting, forecasting, funding sources, financial controls, and reporting mechanisms to ensure the program stays within budget and achieves financial objectives.                                       |         |        |
| Procurement Management Plan                     | - Describes how goods and services will be acquired for the program. Includes procurement strategies, supplier selection criteria, contract management processes, and mechanisms for ensuring timely and cost-effective procurement.                                                   |         |        |
| Quality Management Plan                         | - Outlines the quality standards and processes for the program. Includes quality assurance and control activities to ensure that the program's deliverables meet the required quality standards and stakeholder expectations.                                                          |         |        |
| Resource Management Plan                        | - Details how resources (human, financial, and material) will be allocated and managed within the program. Includes resource planning, allocation, tracking, and optimization strategies to ensure efficient and effective use of resources.                                           |         |        |
| Schedule Management Plan                        | - Describes how the program's schedule will be developed, monitored, and controlled. Includes processes for defining activities, sequencing, estimating durations, and tracking progress to ensure timely completion of the program's objectives.                                      |         |        |
| Scope Management Plan                           | - Outlines how the program's scope will be defined, validated, and controlled. Includes processes for managing scope changes and ensuring that the program's deliverables meet the agreed-upon requirements and objectives.                                                            |         |        |
